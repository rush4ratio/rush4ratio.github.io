The Content Creator - Harleen Sehmi

Harleen captures ideas in words and images. Equipped with three years of practical and theoretical training in Journalism & Media Studies at Rhodes University (South Africa), she can create stories rich in detail and meaning. After specialising in Photojournalism, she attained a postgraduate BA (Honours) degree in media research theory to understand diverse and dynamic media landscapes. Her copywriting prowess is extremely valuable in a world where scannable text and conciseness are prerequisites to communicating successfully.

With a wide range of interests, Harleen easily adapts as a journalist - she has written for community newspapers in South Africa and lifestyle publications in Kenya.

The Technical Implementer - Rush Kirubi

Rush solves problems with design. He has the practical skills to build a variety of web-based software components and the theoretical knowledge to discern patterns as well as data trends; useful in crafting relevant psychological stimuli. Graduating from United States International University (Kenya) summa cum laude in Information Systems & Technology, he is sharpening his analytical skills with an additional qualification as a Bachelor of Economics & Statistics (Kenyatta University, Kenya).

Balancing form and function he reckons the title 'graphic designer' as a misnomer and would rather be considered as a variant of a functional designer. Rush streamlines communication systems according to modern aesthetics.
